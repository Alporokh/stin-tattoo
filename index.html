The artist OUR ARTIST-ANNA

Anna is a tattoo artist whose work is built around individuality, aesthetics, and attention to detail. Her main styles are ornamental, floral, and graphic tattoos — combining clean composition, delicate flow, and strong visual character.

At the same time, she works confidently across many styles, including micro realism, traditional, neo traditional, fine line, blackwork, minimalism, lettering, and more. Whether in color or black and grey, each tattoo is created with care to match the person wearing it.

For her, tattooing is more than just art on skin — it’s a personal story, a feeling, and something that should stay meaningful and beautiful for years to come.

Aftercare

Your tattoo is a fresh wound. Follow these steps carefully for perfect, lasting results.

  1. 01

    Second skin for 4 days. Keep the film on for the first four days without removing it.

  2. 02

    Wash and moisturize. Gently remove the film, wash the tattoo, pat dry with a paper towel, apply the recommended healing cream. Repeat 2–3 times daily for 3 weeks.

  3. 03

    Avoid sun, pools and saunas while healing. Direct sunlight and chlorine damage fresh ink.

  4. 04

    Do not pick or scratch the tattoo. Let it peel naturally.

  5. 05

    Have questions? If something feels wrong or you're unsure about your healing — text us anytime.
